What is Text2Learn?

Knowledge at your fingertips.

Yes, we mean this literally.

With Text2Learn, all a person has to do to get started with their educational goals with us is subscribe to our services. From the get-go, the only thing someone has to do to be a part of our community is text us; in this case, the student would text "SUBSCRIBE" to get started. The moment they do, they are presented with different topics to learn about. We are currently offering students a chance to learn math and/or Spanish.

Our approach to teaching students these subjects is different in that they do not have to strictly follow a specific courseload. Rather, they get to choose when to have a question delivered to them, when to answer a question, and whether or not they even want another question sent to them. We give our students complete control over what they want to learn and when they want to learn it.
