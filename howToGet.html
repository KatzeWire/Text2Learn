Getting Text2Learn

Steps to Subscribe and Use

1. Send "SUBSCRIBE" to +14152149331 to use our services.
2. The first message you get in return should prompt you to reply with "HELPME". Replying with this phrase will give you a list of our commands.
3. Once you send the help message, you should receive another message telling you what courses are offered. Follow the onscreen messages to continue your lesson!
4. Remember when you are answering a question, the first word in your reply should be "ANSWER" followed by your answer.
5. Everytime you answer a question correctly, you will receive 10 points in your name!
6. If you want to guess the word of the day, reply should be "GUESS" followed by your guess on the word of the day.
