Back to the Basics

Unlike many learning apps on the market today, we do not rely on the user having an Internet connection to interact with our app. We are purely SMS-based and so can reach everyone with access to the most basic of cellphones.

Easy Access to Education

Not relying on every person having a smartphone and an Internet connection, our app allows us to connect to those who would otherwise not have the chance at an interactive education that apps bring to us today.

Simple User Controls

There are no unnecessary frills included in our app. We get people the information they want when they want to receive it. Everything our app does is solely up to the user, from what they want to study to when they want new problems to solve.

HackingEDU 2015

The idea and inspiration came from the world's largest hacking convention of its time: HackingEDU 2015. The theme for the hackathon was education. So we, a team of five girls, set out to deliver a practical and affordable application to expand the number of people who can have access to education.

This app is Text2Learn.

Our Work

Text2Learn (T2L) revolves around just sending and receiving text messages. This means that those who do not have access to a smartphone no longer have to worry about not being able to keep up with how fast education is changing due to technology. What Text2Learn does is slow down this change enough that everyone with a basic connection to the world can have as interactive an education as those with access to the Internet 24/7. Now, because of our app, they are able to take part in the new way the world is learning.
